Overview

Cybersecurity Solutions

Protect your business with enterprise-grade cybersecurity solutions from DesertLink IT.

In today's threat landscape, cybersecurity is not optional. DesertLink IT provides comprehensive cybersecurity solutions to organizations in Kenya and East Africa, helping them defend against evolving threats, meet compliance requirements, and protect their critical assets.

Our cybersecurity services cover the full spectrum of security needs: endpoint protection, network security, email security, identity and access management, security audits, vulnerability assessments, and security awareness training. We work with leading security vendors including Microsoft Defender, Fortinet, and other industry-leading platforms.

What We Offer

Security Solutions

Comprehensive cybersecurity solutions tailored to protect your organization.

Endpoint Protection

Microsoft Defender for Endpoint, next-gen antivirus, EDR (Endpoint Detection and Response), device control, application control, and attack surface reduction for all your endpoints.

Firewall & Network Security

Fortinet FortiGate firewalls, next-generation firewall (NGFW) capabilities, intrusion prevention (IPS), VPN, web filtering, and network segmentation for robust perimeter defense.

Email Security

Microsoft Defender for Office 365, anti-phishing, anti-spam, anti-malware, safe attachments, safe links, email encryption, and advanced threat protection for your messaging environment.

Identity & Access Management

Azure AD/Entra ID security, multi-factor authentication (MFA), Conditional Access, Privileged Identity Management (PIM), identity governance, and access reviews.

Security Audits & Assessments

Vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, security posture reviews, compliance audits (ISO 27001, NIST, GDPR), risk assessments, and remediation planning.

Security Awareness Training

Phishing simulations, security awareness programs, employee training modules, policy development, and security culture building to reduce human risk.
