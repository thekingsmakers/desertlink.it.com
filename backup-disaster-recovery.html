Overview

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Comprehensive data protection and business continuity solutions from DesertLink IT.

DesertLink IT helps organizations across Kenya and East Africa safeguard their critical data and applications with enterprise-grade backup and disaster recovery solutions. Our team designs and manages robust protection strategies that minimize downtime, ensure data integrity, and keep your business running through any disruption.

We deliver end-to-end backup and DR services spanning Azure Backup, Azure Site Recovery, Veeam Backup & Replication, on-premises backup infrastructure, disaster recovery planning, and business continuity consulting. From RPO/RTO definition to failover testing and ongoing management, we provide the expertise and tools you need to achieve your recovery objectives.

What We Offer

Backup & DR Offerings

Comprehensive data protection solutions tailored to your organization's needs.

Azure Backup

Enterprise backup for Azure VMs, SQL Server, SAP HANA, Azure Files, and on-premises workloads. Policy-based scheduling, encryption at rest and in transit, and long-term retention with immutable storage.

Azure Site Recovery

Automated disaster recovery and failover for Azure workloads and on-premises environments. Replicate, fail over, and fail back with orchestrated recovery plans and non-disruptive DR drills.

Veeam Backup & Replication

Industry-leading backup for virtual, physical, and cloud environments. Instant VM recovery, granular file-level restore, backup copy jobs, and integration with Azure Blob and S3 for off-site storage.

On-Premises Backup

Traditional backup infrastructure including tape libraries, NAS appliances, and disk-based backup appliances. Integrated with cloud for hybrid tiering and off-site protection.

Disaster Recovery Planning

Comprehensive DR planning including business impact analysis, RPO/RTO definition, recovery strategy design, runbook creation, and regular failover testing to validate readiness.

Business Continuity Consulting

Strategic consulting to align your backup and DR capabilities with business continuity objectives. Risk assessments, capability maturity models, and continuity plan development.
