Ransomware Protection & Recovery

Defend your business against ransomware with layered security, backup strategies, and rapid incident response from DesertLink IT.

Overview

Ransomware Protection & Recovery

Defend your business against one of the most dangerous cyber threats today.

Ransomware attacks are growing in frequency and sophistication, targeting organizations of all sizes across East Africa. These attacks can encrypt your critical data, disrupt operations, and demand significant ransoms. DesertLink IT provides comprehensive ransomware protection services designed to prevent attacks, detect threats early, and ensure rapid recovery when incidents occur.

Our multi-layered approach combines advanced endpoint protection, email security, backup strategies, continuous monitoring, and a proven incident response framework. We help organizations in Kenya and East Africa build resilience against ransomware, minimize downtime, protect sensitive data, and avoid the financial and reputational damage that follows a successful attack.

What We Offer

Ransomware Protection Services

Comprehensive protection across prevention, detection, and response.

Endpoint Protection

Microsoft Defender for Endpoint, EDR, antivirus, and endpoint detection and response. Real-time protection against malware, ransomware, and fileless attacks across all devices.

Email Security

Anti-phishing protection, safe links, safe attachments, and advanced filtering. Block ransomware delivery vectors through email with Microsoft Defender for Office 365.

Backup & Air-Gapped Storage

Immutable backups with air-gapped storage solutions. Azure Backup, offline backups, and the 3-2-1 backup strategy ensuring you can recover data even if ransomware bypasses primary defenses.

Detection & Monitoring

24/7 security monitoring with SIEM and SOC services. Azure Sentinel, Microsoft Defender for Cloud, and advanced analytics for early detection of ransomware indicators.

Incident Response & Containment

Rapid incident response to contain and eradicate ransomware. Forensic analysis, containment strategies, system isolation, and evidence preservation for legal and insurance purposes.

Recovery & Business Continuity

System restoration from clean backups, business continuity planning, disaster recovery testing, and post-incident hardening to prevent recurrence and minimize downtime.
