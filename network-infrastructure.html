Overview

Network Infrastructure

Reliable, secure, and high-performance networking solutions from DesertLink IT.

DesertLink IT delivers enterprise-grade network infrastructure solutions to organizations across Kenya and East Africa. From LAN/WAN design and SD-WAN deployment to structured cabling, wireless networking, and security architecture, our certified engineers build networks that are fast, resilient, and secure.

We partner with industry-leading vendors including Cisco, Fortinet, Ruckus, Aruba, and UniFi to deliver best-in-class networking solutions. Whether you need a new network from the ground up, an upgrade to improve performance and security, or ongoing management and monitoring, DesertLink IT has the expertise to deliver.

What We Offer

Network Offerings

Comprehensive networking solutions tailored to your organization's needs.

LAN/WAN Design

Design and deployment of local and wide area networks using Cisco and Fortinet switches and routers. VLAN segmentation, routing protocols, QoS, and high-availability architectures.

SD-WAN Implementation

Software-defined WAN solutions that optimize connectivity across multiple sites. Application-aware routing, link aggregation, and centralized orchestration with Cisco and Fortinet SD-WAN.

Wireless & Wi-Fi Design

Enterprise wireless networks with Ruckus, Aruba, and UniFi access points. Site surveys, channel planning, seamless roaming, and centralized controller management for optimal coverage.

Structured Cabling

Professional structured cabling infrastructure including Cat6, Cat6A, and fiber optic installations. Rack build-outs, patch panel termination, cable management, and certification testing.

Network Security

Next-generation firewalls, intrusion prevention, VPN, VLAN segmentation, and zero-trust network access. Fortinet FortiGate and Cisco Firepower deployment and management.

Network Monitoring & Management

24/7 network monitoring, performance analytics, alerting, and proactive management. SNMP-based monitoring, NetFlow analysis, bandwidth reporting, and capacity planning.
