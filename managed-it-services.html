Managed IT Services

Proactive managed IT services including remote monitoring, help desk support, patch management, and end user support for businesses in Kenya and East Africa.

Overview

Managed IT Services

Comprehensive IT management and support services that keep your business running smoothly.

DesertLink IT provides comprehensive managed IT services to organizations in Kenya and East Africa. Our proactive approach combines 24/7 remote monitoring, preventive maintenance, responsive help desk support, and dedicated account management to keep your IT environment running at peak performance.

We take ownership of your IT operations so you can focus on your core business. From server and network monitoring to end user support and asset management, our managed services deliver the reliability, security, and support your organization needs to thrive.

What We Offer

Managed IT Offerings

Comprehensive IT management and support solutions for your business.

24/7 Remote Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of servers, networks, workstations, and critical applications. Proactive alerting, automated remediation, and monthly health reports keep you informed and in control.

Help Desk Support

Responsive help desk with ticketing system, remote assistance, phone support, and email support. SLAs tailored to your business needs with fast response and resolution times.

Patch Management

Automated patch deployment for Windows, Linux, and third-party applications. Vulnerability patching, critical security updates, and scheduled maintenance windows to minimize disruption.

End User Support

Desktop and laptop support, mobile device management (MDM), printer support, software installation, email and collaboration tool support, and user onboarding and training.

IT Asset Management

Complete hardware and software asset inventory, license tracking, warranty management, lifecycle planning, procurement management, and asset disposition services.

Preventive Maintenance

Regular system health checks, performance optimization, log analysis, disk cleanup, firmware updates, hardware diagnostics, and capacity planning to prevent issues before they occur.
