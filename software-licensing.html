Overview

Software Licensing Solutions

Navigate the complexities of software licensing with expert guidance from DesertLink IT.

DesertLink IT provides comprehensive software licensing solutions to organizations in Kenya and East Africa. Our licensing experts help you navigate the complex world of Microsoft licensing, ensuring you choose the right programs and agreements for your organization's size and needs.

From Cloud Solution Provider (CSP) and Enterprise Agreements to Open Value and MPSA, we guide you through every licensing model, optimize your spend, and ensure full compliance with Microsoft licensing terms.

What We Offer

Licensing Services

Comprehensive licensing solutions tailored to your organization's needs.

Cloud Solution Provider

Microsoft CSP licensing for Microsoft 365, Azure, Dynamics 365, and standalone online services. Monthly or annual subscriptions with flexible billing and license management through the CSP portal.

Enterprise Agreement & MPSA

Microsoft Enterprise Agreement (EA), Microsoft Product & Services Agreement (MPSA), Open Value, Open License, and Select Plus programs for organizations with 250+ users or devices.

Compliance Audits

License compliance reviews, true-up assessments, SAM engagement support, and audit defense. We help you prepare for and respond to Microsoft license verification requests.

SAM Services

Complete software asset management including inventory discovery, license reconciliation, usage optimization, renewal planning, and cost reduction recommendations.

Subscription Management

End-to-end management of Microsoft subscription lifecycle: provisioning, user management, license assignment, add-on procurement, renewal tracking, and cancellation.

Licensing Advisory

Strategic licensing advice, contract negotiation support, vendor management, licensing health checks, and optimization roadmaps tailored to your organization's growth plans.
