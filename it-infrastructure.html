IT Infrastructure

Enterprise-grade infrastructure solutions including servers, storage, networking, virtualization, and data center services for organizations in Kenya and East Africa.

Overview

IT Infrastructure Solutions

Design, deploy, and manage robust IT infrastructure that powers your business operations.

DesertLink IT delivers end-to-end IT infrastructure solutions to organizations in Kenya and East Africa. Our team designs, deploys, and manages robust infrastructure environments that provide the performance, reliability, and security your business demands.

We partner with leading hardware and software vendors including Dell Technologies, HP Enterprise, Lenovo, Cisco, VMware, and Fortinet to deliver best-in-class infrastructure solutions. From server rooms to full data center deployments, we cover every aspect of your infrastructure needs.

What We Offer

Infrastructure Solutions

Comprehensive infrastructure solutions tailored to your organization's needs.

Server Solutions

Dell PowerEdge and HPE ProLiant servers, tower and rack-mount configurations, server virtualization with VMware vSphere/Hyper-V, and high-performance computing for demanding workloads.

Storage Solutions

SAN, NAS, and DAS storage arrays, all-flash and hybrid storage, software-defined storage, storage area networks, and backup storage appliances from Dell, HPE, and NetApp.

Networking

Cisco and Fortinet switches, routers, firewalls, wireless access points, network controllers, structured cabling, and SD-WAN solutions for reliable, secure connectivity.

Virtualization

VMware vSphere, Microsoft Hyper-V, Nutanix AHV, desktop virtualization with VMware Horizon and Citrix, virtual desktop infrastructure (VDI), and application virtualization.

Data Center

Data center design and build, server room setup, rack and stack, power distribution, cooling, environmental monitoring, and data center migration services.

Backup & Monitoring

Veeam Backup & Replication, infrastructure monitoring with PRTG/Zabbix, backup appliances, tape libraries, backup policies, and 24/7 infrastructure monitoring services.
