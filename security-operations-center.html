Security Operations Center (SOC)

Proactive threat detection and response through DesertLink IT's dedicated Security Operations Center.

Overview

SOC Services

Dedicated security operations to protect your organization around the clock.

A Security Operations Center (SOC) is a centralized function that monitors, detects, analyzes, and responds to cybersecurity incidents using a combination of technology, processes, and skilled personnel. DesertLink IT provides fully managed SOC services to organizations in Kenya and East Africa, giving you enterprise-grade security monitoring without the significant investment in infrastructure and talent.

With DesertLink IT's SOC services, you benefit from continuous monitoring of your networks, endpoints, and cloud environments. Our analysts use advanced SIEM platforms, threat intelligence feeds, and proven incident response procedures to identify and neutralize threats before they impact your business. From log management to compliance reporting, our SOC delivers comprehensive security operations tailored to your organization's risk profile.

Our Process

How We Work

A structured approach to security operations and incident management.

1

Onboard

Integrate your infrastructure with our SOC platform. Deploy log collectors, configure data sources, and establish baseline activity patterns for your environment.

2

Collect

Aggregate logs and security events from all sources including servers, network devices, endpoints, cloud platforms, and applications into our centralized SIEM.

3

Detect

Apply correlation rules, behavioral analytics, and threat intelligence to identify suspicious activity, anomalies, and potential security incidents.

4

Respond

Execute predefined incident response playbooks to contain, eradicate, and recover from threats while keeping stakeholders informed throughout the process.

5

Report

Deliver detailed security reports including incident summaries, trending analysis, SLA performance metrics, and recommendations for security improvement.
