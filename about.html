About DesertLink IT

We are a technology solutions company dedicated to helping businesses across Kenya and East Africa modernize, secure, and optimize their IT infrastructure through tailored, innovative solutions.

Who We Are

Our Story

DesertLink IT was founded with a clear purpose: to bridge the technology gap for businesses in Kenya and across East Africa. We recognized that organizations needed a trusted partner who could provide enterprise-grade technology solutions tailored to their unique challenges, budget, and growth ambitions.

Today, we are a proud IT company in Kenya serving clients across the region with end-to-end technology services. From software procurement and Microsoft licensing to Azure cloud migration, cybersecurity, managed IT services, and custom software development, we deliver solutions that help businesses operate more efficiently, securely, and competitively.

We created DesertLink IT to assist companies throughout their technology journey — from the initial consultation and planning stages through implementation and long-term support. Our tailored approach ensures that every solution we deliver aligns with our clients' specific business objectives, industry requirements, and growth trajectory.

What sets us apart is our commitment to understanding each client's business before recommending any solution. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all technology. Instead, we take the time to assess your current infrastructure, identify pain points, and design a customized roadmap that delivers measurable results.
