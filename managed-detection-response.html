Overview

Managed Detection & Response

24/7 threat detection and response to protect your organization from cyberattacks.

Managed Detection and Response (MDR) is a managed security service that combines advanced technology with human expertise to detect, investigate, and respond to cyber threats in real time. DesertLink IT's MDR service provides organizations in Kenya and East Africa with round-the-clock security monitoring without the overhead of building and staffing an in-house Security Operations Center (SOC).

Cyber threats are evolving faster than ever, and traditional perimeter defenses alone are no longer sufficient. MDR bridges the gap by continuously monitoring your environment for suspicious activity, conducting proactive threat hunting, and rapidly containing incidents before they escalate. With DesertLink IT's MDR, you gain access to skilled security analysts, advanced detection tools, and proven incident response procedures that keep your business safe.

What We Offer

MDR Offerings

Comprehensive detection and response capabilities tailored to your environment.

24/7 SOC Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of your networks, endpoints, and cloud environments by DesertLink IT's security analysts. Real-time alert triage and escalation ensures no threat goes unnoticed.

Threat Hunting & Intelligence

Proactive threat hunting using the latest threat intelligence feeds, IoCs, and behavioral analytics to identify hidden threats that evade automated detection systems.

Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR)

Deploy and manage EDR agents across all endpoints for real-time visibility, threat detection, and automated response to malicious activity on desktops, laptops, and servers.

Network Detection & Response (NDR)

Network traffic analysis using machine learning and behavioral models to detect lateral movement, data exfiltration, and command-and-control communications.

Incident Investigation & Forensics

In-depth forensic investigation of security incidents including root cause analysis, timeline reconstruction, evidence collection, and remediation recommendations.

SIEM Management & Tuning

Deployment, configuration, and ongoing tuning of Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) platforms to reduce noise and improve detection accuracy.

Our Process

How We Work

A structured approach to threat detection and incident response.

1

Onboarding

Deploy sensors, agents, and integrate your existing security tools. Configure data sources and establish baseline behavior for your environment.

2

Detection
